Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal information and privacy is our top priority at QuickCert

1. Introduction

QuickCert is the official digital platform of the Divisional Secretariat of Sri Lanka, designed to streamline the income statement certification process. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and disclose your information when you use our online certification services.

By using QuickCert, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information necessary for income statement certification:

  • National Identity Card (NIC) number
  • Full name as per official records
  • Email address for communication and verification
  • Mobile phone number for SMS notifications
  • Residential address
  • Employment and pension details
  • Bank account information (for income verification)

2.2 Financial Information

To process your income statement certification, we collect:

  • Pension payment records from external government databases
  • Salary and allowance details
  • Deduction information
  • Net income calculations

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information including:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Login timestamps and access logs
  • Application usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Processing income statement certification requests
  • Verifying your identity and eligibility
  • Communicating application status updates
  • Generating digitally signed income certificates
  • Maintaining audit trails for government compliance
  • Providing customer support and assistance
  • Improving our services and user experience

Important: We never use your personal information for commercial purposes, marketing, or any activities outside of official government certification processes.

4. Data Security and Protection

4.1 Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data storage
  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication (2FA) for enhanced security
  • Role-based access control for system users
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with automated backups

4.2 Digital Signatures and Document Security

All generated income certificates include:

  • Digital signatures for authenticity verification
  • QR codes for document validation
  • Tamper-proof PDF generation
  • Secure document storage and access controls

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Government Agencies

We may share your information with:

  • Divisional Secretariat offices for verification purposes
  • Relevant government departments as required by law
  • Authorized officers for application processing

5.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers for:

  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure services
  • Email and SMS notification services
  • Payment processing (when applicable)

All third-party providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and comply with government data protection standards.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Active Applications: Until processing is complete
  • Completed Applications: 7 years as per government record-keeping requirements
  • User Accounts: Until account deletion is requested
  • Audit Logs: 5 years for compliance and security purposes

After the retention period, all personal data is securely deleted using government-approved data destruction methods.

7. Your Rights and Choices

As a QuickCert user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information stored in our system
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Download copies of your income certificates
  • Track the status of your applications
  • Request account deactivation (subject to legal requirements)
  • Receive notifications about policy changes

7.1 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Online Tracking

QuickCert uses essential cookies and tracking technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session securely
  • Remember your application progress
  • Ensure system security and prevent fraud
  • Improve system performance and user experience

We do not use cookies for advertising or non-essential tracking purposes.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or service improvements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Notify registered users via email
  • Display a notice on the QuickCert platform
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this policy
