Welcome to QuickCert

Your Gateway to Fast Income Statement Certification

QuickCert is the official digital platform of the Divisional Secretariat of Sri Lanka, designed to streamline the income statement certification process. Get your income certificates quickly, securely, and reliably through our user-friendly online system.
